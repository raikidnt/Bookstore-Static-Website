Adventure is a genre of literature that features stories of adventure, such as world travel, difficult quests, voyages of discovery, and other journeys. Adventure stories usually involve a character who is brought to the adventure by chance, and chance usually plays a large role in the episodes of the story.

Historical Adventure

Historical adventure books are exciting fiction stories set in a historical time and place, featuring daring journeys, quests, or missions with a mix of real historical events and fictional plot elements.

Action Adventure

Action adventure books are fast-paced fiction stories with thrilling physical action and high stakes, featuring a hero or group of heroes battling against a powerful antagonist or force.

Fantasy Adventure

Fantasy adventure books feature heroes on a journey in a magical world with action, danger, and suspense as they strive to overcome obstacles and save their world.
