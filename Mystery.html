Mystery is a genre of literature whose stories focus on a puzzling crime, situation, or circumstance that needs to be solved. The central character is often a detective who eventually solves the mystery by logical deduction from facts presented to the reader

Detective Mystery

Detective mysteries typically feature a crime or puzzle that needs to be solved by a detective or amateur sleuth. The focus is on uncovering clues, piecing together evidence, and ultimately solving the mystery.

Police Procedural

Police Procedural is a realistic mystery subgenre that focuses on the technical aspects of criminal investigations, featuring a team of detectives working together to solve a case with attention to accurate details and procedures.

Historical Mystery

Historical Mystery is a mystery subgenre set in a historical period or setting. These stories include accurate details of the time period and revolve around solving a crime or uncovering a mystery within that context.
