Mystery

Mystery is a genre of literature whose stories focus on a puzzling crime, situation, or circumstance that needs to be solved. The central character is often a detective who eventually solves the mystery by logical deduction from facts presented to the reader

Adventure

Adventure is a genre of literature that features stories of adventure, such as world travel, difficult quests, voyages of discovery, and other journeys. Adventure stories usually involve a character who is brought to the adventure by chance, and chance usually plays a large role in the episodes of the story.

Fiction

Fiction is a genre of literature that encompasses a wide range of imaginative and invented stories, presented in book form. This genre is focused on storytelling that is not based on actual events or people, allowing authors to create worlds, characters, and situations that explore different themes and ideas.
